ROLE: VIDEO PRODUCTION

DELIVERABLE: VIDEO

DATE: JAN 2017

monopo produced the campaign movie for the latest Adidas | MOUSSY collaboration.

BRIEF

“SCENELESS SPORTS”

The collaboration between Adidas and MOUSSY formed the concept of SCENELESS SPORTS, a clothing line equally fashionable and stylish but with the ease of movement of a sportswear collection. They needed a campaign movie reflecting these two aspects of the collection: fashionable and functional.

ART DIRECTION

Symmetrical & Geometrical compositions mixing Sports and Lifestyle feeling

Our aesthetic is based on symmetrical compositions and geometrical shapes, mixing footage of sports arena and fashion model. We used the kinetic lines of “A” for Adidas and “M” for MOUSSY, “|” for collaboration and the 3 lines of Adidas logo as inspiration for our compositions.
